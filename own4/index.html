ABOUT ARTIST

WHAT'S MY
BACKGROUND?

The band reunited in 2007 after several months of travelling together, as the band traveled to a foreign country for their concert in Rome in August 2007. At the end of 2008, they recorded their first album. It was released on the band's second label, The Cinémathème, but it had been shelved due to issues associated with French licensing. The band returned to the States and joined their third label, The Aulté du Chartres, which re-opened in August.
